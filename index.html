Story

For six generations, the kingdom has been controlled by a steward. Even royal lineage suitors are barred from claiming the throne unless they hold the King's Scepter, according to the law. Prince Deuterimus just found the scepter had been purposefully buried in an old temple. Said temple was located on a small island that was mysteriously sunk to the ocean's depths. The location has been totally flooded. The prince dispatched a team of treasure hunters to find the scepter, but they never returned. The area is teeming with nasty sea monsters. Prince Deuterimus is on the lookout for a strong person to help him reclaim the scepter. They would gain the favor of the kingdom's future monarch if they were successful.
